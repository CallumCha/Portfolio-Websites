Overview
During my internship at Micro Nav Ltd, I led the UI/UX design for the BEST Insights app, an advanced air traffic control simulation tool. This role involved developing user-friendly and intuitive interfaces for several critical components of the app. Additionally, I created high-fidelity prototypes using Figma, ensuring each design was aligned with the project’s objectives and user needs. 

My first task at MicroNav was to work with the Product Manager to create some concept designs for Best Insights, which is a sophisticated tool designed to enhance air traffic control training by providing rich, data-driven insights. It allows for the analysis and replay of training sessions with integrated instructor feedback, viewable on any device, which supports both on-site and remote learning. Additionally, BEST Insights offers powerful features for operational analysis, making it a versatile tool for improving both training and everyday operations in air traffic management​.

After going over design requirements and working with the Product Manager we settled on some designs he was happy with. From there I started work on the overall theme and details on individual parts of the application.   

I managed all of the project within FIgma where I split each tool within the app into its own page where I created designs that I then worked on with the product manager.
